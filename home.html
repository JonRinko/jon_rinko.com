About Me

I am currently an Analyst in the private sector and a volunteer member of the Financial and Statistical reporting team for the Redcross.
I completed my undergrate education at Rider University, in Lawrenceville NJ, where I studied Finance. My passion for technology led me to the Rutgers Data Science Bootcamp, where I learned much about programming and data science.
